About Me

I’m a blue-teamer at heart who likes to build things

Here’s a longer, more official-sounding version:

Josh Stepp is a cybersecurity professional with over a decade of experience in threat hunting, detection engineering, and system architecture. A proud veteran of the United States Air Force, where he had the opportunity to serve his nation as a Cyberwarfare Operator. As the creator of the IntrusionsInDepth Podcast, Josh analyzes cyber attacks and the threat actors that conduct them. When not behind the keyboard, he can be found on the Jiu-Jitsu mats, in the gym, or outside with his family.
