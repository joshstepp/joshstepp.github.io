About

I'm a blue-teamer at heart who likes to build things.

Josh Stepp is a cybersecurity professional with over a decade of experience in threat hunting, detection engineering, and system architecture. A proud veteran of the United States Air Force, where he served as a Cyberwarfare Operator.

These days Josh Stepp is a Regional Services Director at Elastic for the US PUBSEC Team where he helps some of the most important organizations in the world execute their mission, secure critical infrastructure and win using Elastic.

As the creator of the IntrusionsInDepth Podcast, Josh analyzes cyber attacks and the threat actors that conduct them. When not behind the keyboard, he can be found on the Jiu-Jitsu mats, in the gym, or outside with his family.
